What is design?

It's a mapping of the requirements to the underlying languages,
libraries, frameworks, etc. to solve the given problem.

What is a good design?
-is relevant
-easy to change and maintain
-is extensible
-is understandable
-is modular
-is reversible?
-is simple
-is cost effective
-high cohesion
-low coupling
-*testable
-minimizes the frequency and impact of change
-robust and resilient
-...

Economic impact of our design decisions

A good design is not the one that correctly predicts the future, but
the one that makes the changing to accommodate the needs in the
future more affordable.

Code is like people on a crowded train
